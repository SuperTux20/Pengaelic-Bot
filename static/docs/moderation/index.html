Most of these staff tools are fairly universal. "Drama" refers to the drama role/channel, like an isolated private channel for someone on their last chance before they're given the boot, this is configurable in the options.

p!<kick/ban/mute/drama/warn> <user>

p!suggestion <approve/deny/consider/implement> <suggestion ID> [reason for tagging the suggestion]
